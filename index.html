Pilot Project for Jamaica

Lack of automation, track and trace and the high cost of transporting import and export goods continue to impede economic growth for low-income countries. Shippinghub Global’s sole objective (not-for-profit) is to provide an information infrastructure and industry-proven logistics automation solutions to assist developing countries such as Jamaica to control and lower their future transportation costs.

"Improved internal logistics can and will help feed millions"

ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy (SNLS) has earned support and commitment from our Canadian municipal, provincial and federal government officials along with private and public sector organizations in Jamaica.

Transform the entire island into a logistics hub

With the launch of ShippingHub Control Tower and ShippingHub GEO View powered by NACgeo, we are more ready than ever to implement SNLS pilot project for Jamaica to “Transform the entire island into a logistics hub”, the foundation for growth, competitiveness and a logistics-centered economy.
