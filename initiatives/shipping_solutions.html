Automated Shipping Solutions


We wish to provide industry-proven automated shipping solutions with full track-and-trace capabilities to all Jamaican shippers.

The primary objective of shipping automation is to keep information and product flow synchronized. Our automated shipping solutions will improve both productivity and visibility by allowing everyone in the supply chain to securely share information with one another.

ShippingHub will provide ready-to-deploy server and web-based shipping solutions. Shippers will be able to select one or both of these options.

  • The ShippingHub server-based shipping solution is designed for high-volume shippers with a desire for speed, and for users where internet access is limited.

  • The web-based shipping solution is ideal for users needing external access to shipping rates and data.


Features and Benefits of ShippingHub Automated Shipping Solutions


  • ShippingHub Dashboard and Visualization Tool powered by NACgeo
    Information captured or imported into the ShippingHub information infrastructure will be instantly available for analysis through the ShippingHub Dashboard and ShippingHub Visualization Tool powered by NACgeo. The Dashboard will also provide the opportunity for multiple shippers to save on transportation costs by facilitating their collective consolidation of goods for shipment.

  • Template Manager
    The web-based shipping solution includes the ShippingHub Template Manager, allowing users to easily create, change and access multiple data entry templates. These entry templates can be easily adapted to the constantly changing requirements of shipment processing without the necessity for programming changes.

  • Track and Trace
    ShippingHub includes complete track-and-trace capabilities for tracking shipments, especially of agricultural products.

  • Cost Simulator
    High volume shippers may use the Cost Simulator feature to perform 'what-if' scenarios to further save on transportation costs.

  • Request API calls
    This feature is also available to easily interface with other software applications with full Transportation Management System (TMS) capabilities such as: SHOP, SHIP, CANCEL, EOD and TRACK.

  • Port Community System compatibility
    With the ShippingHub information infrastructureâ€™s smooth ability to interface with other systems, the ShippingHub Dashboard can be easily configured to connect to Jamaica's up-coming Port Community System.

  • Compliant Shipping Document Preparation
    Prepare all compliant shipping documents such as labels, waybills, manifests, customs declarations, and dangerous goods declarations.

  • Transportation Provider Compliant
    ShippingHub Automated ShippingHub Solutions come with a ready-to-ship assurance, certified by, or compliant with, most major transportation providers to the United States, Canada and international destinations. This includes transportation providers such as UPS, DHL, FedEx, USPS, Canada Post, and more.

Our automated solution will lower transport costs and simplify international shipping, promoting exports and expanding global trade in the process. We will continuously improve these shipping solutions in order to achieve our objective of removing barriers for exporters.
