ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy (SNLS) Overview

ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy (SNLS)


ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy is a comprehensive ecosystem of solutions consisting of multiple related projects and initiatives. Each one will have a tremendous positive impact on its own.

  • Automated Shipping Solutions for all enterprises to improve on productivity with full Track and Trace capabilities, providing the foundation for shipment consolidations from multiple shippers and creating savings on transportation costs.

  • ShippingHub BIG DATA with NAC (Natural Area Code) and Visualization Tool will provide Track and Trace for crops to increase food security. BIG DATA is an information infrastructure and software application development tool designed to capture, store, process, and present data of all kinds, including data for transportation, energy monitoring, and the ShippingHub Co-op. The NAC represents the ability to give everyone an address for their location, critical in areas that currently have no address system in place.

  • e-ACT (Employment Agencies Collaboration and Training) to create logistics automation information seminars and training programs to promote efficient supply chain execution and to provide job skills training required in a logistics-centered economy.

  • ShippingHub Co-op Management Corporation to help create and administer farming co-operatives to minimize spoilage and maximize profit for farmers with the shortest route and time to market.

  • Cold-chain logistics with ShippingHub Pods and Trailers to extend shelf life of perishables and pave the way for transforming food crops into export crops.

  • Direct-Trade-Route will further reduce and control future transportation costs, thereby promoting and expanding trade. Local businesses and consumers will gain access to industrial and lifestyle products from abroad. The pilot route we’ve identified will be between Kingston, Jamaica and Canada, by air via Toronto and by sea via Halifax.

  • ShippingHub SuperDC / Trans-shipment Centre for collecting inbound and outbound shipment consolidations, facilitating further reduced costs.

Our vision is grand, revolutionary, and transformative – yet very achievable.
