ShippingHub BIG DATA with NAC and Visualization Tool


BIG DATA is a centralized information infrastructure and software application development tool designed to capture, store, process, and present data of all kinds, including data for transportation, energy monitoring, and the ShippingHub Co-op. As a software application development tool for data of all kinds, it will allow developers or students to focus on creating the tools required to PROCESS data, while the ability to CAPTURE, STORE and PRESENT data already exists in forms that are easily configurable without programming changes.

The NAC (Natural Area Code) is a geocode, and represents the ability to give everyone an address for their location, critical in areas that currently have no address system in place. This feature overcomes the barriers to shipping goods in areas that do not have a well-developed national infrastructure for addresses and postal codes.
