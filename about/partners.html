Our Partners and Supporters

In Canada, we have obtained the support of government officials in the City of Markham, the Ontario Provincial Government, and the Government of Canada. We are also honoured to be profiled on InnovationScan, a Canadian Trade Commissioner Service website promoting a select few promising companies.

In Jamaica, we have earned support from private and public sector organizations such as the Jamaica Chamber of Commerce (JCC), the Jamaica Business Development Corporation (JBDC) and the University of Technology, Jamaica (UTech SCIT) to deliver the ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy (SNLS) pilot project for Jamaica.

Please join our amazing journey to ‘democratize’ global trade!
