The Background to ShippingHub Global



ShippingHub Global (SHG) was established by UFMS Inc., a privately held company based in Markham, Ontario, Canada, and founded by Fred K. Wong.

UFMS Inc. has been providing automated shipping solutions to both shippers and transportation providers since 1992. We have extensive industry knowledge, with more than 1,000 organizations across North America relying on our robust, industry proven Transportation Management System (TMS) to process their daily shipments.

With UFMS’ Automated Shipping Solutions as the foundation, ShippingHub was conceived in May, 2012, as an information infrastructure to securely share shipment data with internal and external users throughout the supply chain. Since then, ShippingHub has evolved into a powerful ecosystem with multiple entities and partners.

Appreciating the power of our solutions, we registered ShippingHub Global (SHG) as a non-profit organization to promote the ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy (SNLS) to middle and low-income countries. The core objectives of the SNLS are to lower transportation costs and to improve a country’s internal logistics. Through meeting these objectives, we hope to spur sustainable economic development, and reduce hunger, poverty, and unemployment.
