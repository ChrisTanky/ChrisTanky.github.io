Founder - Mr. Fred K. Wong


Fred consulted as an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) software specialist for nine years to major industry leaders prior to the founding of UFMS Inc. in 1992. Fred is known in the industry to be an innovative thinker and technical problem solver. He has guided his team in designing and implementing numerous significant process improvement projects for UFMS clients. Fred’s notable talent is his unique analytical skills and his constant desire to improve the user experience of the UFMS product suite. Fred holds a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science from the University of Toronto.

How do you manage over 30,000 farmers without automation?


In 2006, the head of a Cameroon coffee co-operative asked Fred this question: “How do you manage over 30,000 farmers without automation?” Inspired by the challenge, Fred began researching an answer. As he did so, he began to appreciate the profound role inadequate logistics played as a barrier to advancing development in low and middle income countries. He also began to see a role for UFMS technologies in solving these problems, and to evolve a strategy that would help low and middle income countries overleap cost barriers to trade and, in doing so, reduce hunger and poverty through the resulting acceleration in economic development.

The result was ShippingHub Global, a not-for-profit company created to market and deliver the ShippingHub National Logistics Strategy (SNLS).
