6th Biennial Jamaican Diaspora Conference 2015

Shippinghub Global

The 6th Biennial Jamaica Diaspora Conference 2015 under the theme "Jamaica and the Diaspora: Linking for Growth and Prosperity" promises to be an exhilarating, focused and not to be missed event

The Conference, convened by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Foreign Trade, is the global forum that connects Jamaicans from all over the world with Jamaicans in the home country every two years. The event seeks to strengthen existing linkages and networks, and to build alliances for Jamaica's development and standing in the world.
